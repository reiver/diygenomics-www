DIY Genomics

This is a site to help those interested in doing DIY genomics.

Getting Started

When people first get into DIY genomics, they often start with their own data. So, for exampe, they have already had their DNA scanned using something like 23andMe, FamilyTreeDNA, or others; have downloaded their raw DNA file; and have started off by using the various tools out there, which you can apply to your DNA.
